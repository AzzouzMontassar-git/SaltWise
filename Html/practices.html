Agroecological Solutions

Soil & Water Management

Gypsum Application MENASouth Asia

How it works: Apply agricultural gypsum (2-4 tons/acre) to displace sodium.

Regional Tip (Egypt): Apply before Nile flooding season (August).

Agricultural gypsum, spreader
3-4 weeks before planting

Sand Mulching Latin AmericaSub-Saharan

How it works: 5cm sand layer reduces evaporation and salt accumulation.

Regional Tip (Chile): Combine with fog nets in Atacama region.

Coarse sand, digging tools
Apply during dry season

Salt-Water Leaching South AsiaMENA

How it works: Flood fields with 6-12" freshwater to flush salts.

Regional Tip (Bangladesh): Time with monsoon rains.

Freshwater source, drainage
Post-harvest season

Rice Husk Mulching South AsiaSub-Saharan

How it works: 3-inch layer reduces evaporation, adds silica.

Regional Tip (Nigeria): Combine with poultry manure.

Rice husks, compost
At planting time

Halophyte Rotation Latin AmericaMENA

How it works: Grow salt-accumulating plants for 1 season.

Regional Tip (Mexico): Use Salicornia as cash crop.

Saltbush seeds
During fallow periods

Raised Bed Planting South AsiaSub-Saharan

How it works: 12" high beds prevent capillary salt rise.

Regional Tip (Senegal): Space beds 50cm apart.

Hoe, measuring stick
Before rainy season

Sulfur Amendment South AsiaMENA

How it works: Elemental sulfur lowers pH in alkaline soils.

Regional Tip (India): Use 300kg/ha in Rajasthan.

Sulfur powder, spreader
3 months before planting

Agroforestry Windbreaks Sub-SaharanLatin America

How it works: Salt-tolerant trees reduce wind erosion.

Regional Tip (Brazil): Use Atriplex nummularia.

Tree saplings, fencing
Plant at start of rains

Compost Tea Irrigation All Regions

How it works: Aerated compost extracts boost microbes.

Regional Tip: Use local manure sources.

Compost, brewing system
Every 2 weeks in season
